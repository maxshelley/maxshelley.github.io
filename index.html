Max Shelley

Designer & Developer.

UI/UX Design & Development and Conversion Optimisation.

Based in South East England, Max has been working on the web for over a decade. Specialising in data-driven design and analysis, he helps clients make the most of their web presence with a determination to provide the best experience for their customers. He has been lucky enough to work with multi-national companies as well as small local retailers in a wide range of sectors.

Alongside his work for clients as part of Measured Brilliance, the agency he runs with his sister, Sam, Max runs Echoleft. A website that helps people prepare for the end of their lives, helps family and friends create beautiful memorial websites and allows charities to raise money through memorial donations.

  • eCommerce Design & Development
  • Marketing & Advertising - Integrating Online & Offline.
